ElanelSystem is an internal business platform built for Elanel Servicios SRL to support day-to-day operations. The system focuses on practical workflows used by the business team, with an emphasis on reliability, traceability, and clear administrative tools.

This repository contains a Django-based application, a server-side rendered UI (HTML/CSS/Vanilla JS), and a Dockerized setup for reproducible environments.

## What this system is used for

- Centralizing operational data related to sales, commissions, and internal management.
- Supporting staff workflows through admin-first interfaces.
- Providing structured reporting to reduce manual calculations and spreadsheet-based processes.
- Helping maintain consistent business rules over time (e.g., commission logic, liquidation cycles).
